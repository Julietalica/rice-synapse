ABOUT SYNAPSE

Synapse is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing connections and support for individuals with brain injuries and raising awareness about brain injuries. Here at Rice Synapse, we aim to provide Rice students with an opportunity to serve as leaders within the local community and learn about the patient perspective of living with a brain injury, but also to provide Rice students who themselves have brain injuries with support, exposure to additional resources, and the opportunity to connect with and learn from one another.
