RICE SYNAPSE

Rice Synapse is a student-run organization dedicated to combating the isolation individuals with brain injury often face through the creation of a powerful social support network at Rice University.

PEER SUPPORT

Peer support group meetings bring together individuals with brain injury, allowing them to share experiences and resources with each other while building meaningful social connections.

BUDDY PROGRAM

Each individual with a brain injury is paired with a Rice student, fostering personal connections and mutual support and reducing the social isolation individuals with a brain injury often experience.

JOIN

Are you interested in joining Rice Synapse? We just launched this year and we looking for members who are interested in helping those who may be affected by a traumatic brain injury.

ABOUT US

We aim to provide Rice students with an opportunity to serve as leaders within the local community and learn about the patient perspective of living with a brain injury, but also to provide Rice students who themselves have brain injuries with support, exposure to additional resources, and the opportunity to connect with and learn from one another.
